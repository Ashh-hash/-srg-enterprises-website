Our Story

From Jaipur to
Every Corner of India

Founded in 2023 and headquartered in the industrial heart of Jhotwara, Jaipur, Shree Radha Govind Enterprises (SRG Enterprises) was established to address a critical gap in the heavy machinery market.

Led by Mr. Sanjay Sharma, Managing Director and Sole Proprietor, we set out to manufacture precision-engineered components that harmonize extreme durability with operational cost-efficiency.

In just a few years, SRG Enterprises has transcended regional boundaries to become a National Supply Chain Partner — serving clients from the mining belts of Odisha to the agricultural heartlands of Punjab.

Core Specializations

Two Sectors. One Standard of Excellence.

We serve the most demanding industries in India with parts engineered to outlast and outperform.

Crusher Machinery Parts

Heavy-duty components for mining, construction & stone crushing operations

We specialize in high-manganese and alloy steel casting for the mining and construction industries. Our parts are designed using advanced metallurgical standards to provide maximum wear resistance and optimized crushing geometry — lowering cost-per-ton for our clients.

Jaw Plates Wiremesh Conveyor Rollers Drum Pulley Gear Box Cone Mentals Roller Stand Rubber Roller Screws & Bolts

Agricultural Tillage Tools

Precision parts for tractors, threshers, harrows & cultivation equipment

Recognizing India as an agrarian powerhouse, we manufacture specialized tillage tools and tractor spares for the farming heartlands of UP, MP, and Maharashtra. Our tools are heat-treated to ensure they never deform — reducing tractor load, fuel costs, and maintenance.
